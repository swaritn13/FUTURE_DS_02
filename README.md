# FUTURE_DS_02
This project focuses on Customer Attrition Analysis for a subscription-based business. Using Power BI, I transformed raw customer data into an interactive dashboard to identify "Churn Triggers"—the specific reasons why customers leave the service.
🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
Tool: Microsoft Power BI Desktop

Data Cleaning: Power Query (Handling nulls, data type conversion, and error removal)

Analytics: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for custom KPI measures

Dashboard Design: Strategic layout for "at-a-glance" business decision-making

🔍 Key Business Insights
After analyzing over 7,000 records, the following high-impact insights were discovered:

The Contract Effect: Customers on Month-to-month contracts are the highest risk, with a churn rate of 42.7%. Encouraging users to switch to 1-year or 2-year contracts could reduce churn by up to 90%.

Technical Friction: Fiber Optic users churn at double the rate (41.9%) of DSL users (18.9%). This suggests a potential service reliability or pricing issue in the high-speed tier.

Payment Method Risk: Users paying by Electronic Check show the highest churn (45.3%), indicating potential friction in the manual payment process.

💡 Strategic Recommendations
Incentivize Loyalty: Launch a marketing campaign to move monthly subscribers to annual plans with a small discount.

Service Audit: Conduct a technical review of the Fiber Optic infrastructure to improve stability and customer satisfaction.

Automate Payments: Encourage customers to switch to Automatic Credit Card or Bank Transfer payments to reduce involuntary churn.
